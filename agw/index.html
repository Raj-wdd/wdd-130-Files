Awareness on Global Warming Site Plan

Global warming is one of the most serious challenges facing humanity today. It refers to the long-term rise in Earth's average surface temperature due to human activities, primarily the emission of greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide. This warming leads to a range of environmental issues, including melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather events. It is critical to raise awareness and take action to combat this global crisis.

Our planet is in crisis. Climate change, driven by human activities, is causing widespread environmental degradation and social disruption. The consequences are far-reaching, affecting everything from extreme weather events to food security and public health. This website is dedicated to raising awareness about the urgent need to address climate change and to empower individuals to take action.
